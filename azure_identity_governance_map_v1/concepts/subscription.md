# Subscription

## What it is
A subscription is an administrative and billing boundary in Azure.

## Why it matters
Many governance and access decisions are applied at subscription scope.

## Security relevance
- Common scope for RBAC and Policy
- Helps separate workloads and responsibilities
- Important for organizing environments

## Related concepts
- RBAC
- Azure Policy
- Resource Group
